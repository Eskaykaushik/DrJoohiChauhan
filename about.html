About

Biography

Dr. Joohi Chauhan is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology (MNNIT) Allahabad since July 2021.

She specializes in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Computer Vision applications for Healthcare and Medical Image Analysis. Her work focuses on developing AI solutions for early disease detection and diagnosis.
